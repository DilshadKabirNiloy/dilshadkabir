My Process

Simple. Proven. Results-focused.

1. Audit

I analyze your current marketing setup — ads, funnels, analytics, and conversion paths — to identify exactly where you're losing potential customers and revenue.

2. Strategize

Based on the audit, I build a custom roadmap — prioritized actions, realistic timelines, and clear KPIs — designed to maximize ROI and eliminate wasted spend.

3. Execute

I launch, monitor, and continuously optimize campaigns with data-driven decisions — testing, refining, and scaling what works until consistent growth becomes predictable.

About Me

I'm a certified Brand Growth & Paid Ads Conversion Specialist and a full-stack digital marketer with a Computer Science background. I approach marketing the way an engineer approaches systems — with structure, logic, and measurable outcomes. Alongside my marketing work, I'm a CS student with a strong interest in AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP), which shapes how I think about data, automation, and performance at scale.

What I do is simple in principle and precise in execution: I help businesses build strong brands and scale revenue through paid advertising, conversion strategy, and data-driven decision-making. I'm certified in Google Ads, Meta Ads, and SEO, and I design campaigns as complete systems — where targeting, messaging, creatives, funnels, and analytics work together to drive consistent, profitable growth. I don't run ads in isolation; I build growth engines that convert attention into customers.

You should hire me if you're looking for more than traffic or short-term wins. I work with brands that want clarity, performance, and scalability — brands that care about ROI today and brand value tomorrow. My focus is on building marketing systems that scale efficiently, reduce wasted spend, and strengthen long-term brand presence while delivering measurable results.
